About Me

I am a computer scientist working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and cognitive science. I am interested in using a combination of theory, data and psychological theories to inform the design of human-aware algorithms and interfaces.

I received my PhD (2015-2020) from the Department of Computer Science at Tulane University, where I was supervised by K. Brent Venable.
